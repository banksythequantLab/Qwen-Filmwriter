I gave an AI one sentence. 24 minutes later it handed me a finished 2-minute film — written, shot, scored, checked & cut by a crew of 8 agents on Qwen Cloud. No human in the loop.

Here's how we built Filmwriter 🧵

------------------------------------------------------------
2/
Every text-to-video demo stops at ONE clip.

But a film is thousands of decisions: how many scenes, long take or montage, does your lead still look like your lead in shot 7?

We wanted AI agents to make ALL of them — and fix their own mistakes.

------------------------------------------------------------
3/  [image: architecture.png]
Filmwriter runs on 9 models on Qwen Cloud (DashScope):

qwen-max + qwen-plus plan & write · qwen3-vl runs visual + legal QA · qwen-image/-edit for stills · wan2.6 for video · qwen3-tts narrates · qwen3-asr checks the read-back.

------------------------------------------------------------
4/  [image: agentcards_contact.png  — the 8-agent lineup]
A conductor orchestrates a crew of 8 agents: Showrunner, Screenwriter, Story Editor, Storyboard, Continuity, Legal & Clearances, Cinematographer, Editor.

Each inspects its own work, flags the exact violation, and re-shoots until it clears QC.

------------------------------------------------------------
5/  [image: pointcards/point_09_full.png  — the 9->3 stat]
Continuity nearly broke us. Reference-anchoring hit diminishing returns fast.

What worked: a streaming re-roll where the crew catches its own drift. Over 7 runs of the same logline, continuity breaks fell from 9 → 3.

------------------------------------------------------------
6/
The honest part: iteration isn't a straight line. Some loops made it WORSE.

And every fix is another model call — so quality has a price. Balancing quality vs cost was the real engineering problem, not the models.

------------------------------------------------------------
7/
What makes it a *showrunner*, not a clip generator: a finished film banks its cast in a "season vault."

Ask for another episode and the same character walks back on set. One-off clips become a series.

------------------------------------------------------------
8/
It's honestly not fully baked — the hardest shots still slip. But that's the thesis: every pass makes it better, and it grades its own progress.

Built on Qwen Cloud / Alibaba Cloud in ~3 weeks.
